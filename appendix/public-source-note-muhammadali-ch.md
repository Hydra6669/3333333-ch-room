# Public Source Note: muhammadali.ch

## What this source is useful for

The site `muhammadali.ch` is useful as a public-facing corroboration layer for the Zurich 1971 asset universe.

It supports:

- public discoverability of the Zurich 1971 fight and visit
- public presentation of the film `The Baddest Daddy in the Whole World`
- named creative and archival roles around the film and photo materials
- visible archive depth through posters, tickets, press material, photographs, and memorabilia
- a public-facing connection between the Eric Bachmann archive, Dominik Bachmann, and the wider Zurich 1971 documentation effort

The site is a corroborative public source, not a legal anchor.
